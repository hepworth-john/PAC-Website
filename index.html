Our Mission

We are a collaborative partnership for pediatric families affected by Cystic Fibrosis and the Primary Children's CF Center — working together to improve the lives of all those living with CF.

What We Do

Programs & Initiatives

The PAC runs year-round programs to support CF families and improve the care experience at Primary Children's.

🌙

Parents Night Out

Quarterly events bringing CF families together for connection, education, and community — in person and virtually.

🤝

Connection Events

Regional gatherings for families in Idaho and Southern Utah to build local support networks.

🎁

Special Programs

Newborn kits for newly diagnosed families and inpatient gifts to bring comfort during hospital stays.

🎄

Sub for Santa

Annual holiday program coordinating gift giving for CF families in need during the holiday season.

📈

Quality Improvement

Partnering with the CF clinic team to improve patient experience in both inpatient and outpatient settings.

💛

CF Foundation

Collaborating with the UT/ID CF Foundation chapter to support local events like Great Strides and Cycle to the Sea.
